US GOV Game Rules

1. Use the WASD keys or arrow keys to move.

2. Press the Shift key to lock your cursor.

3. To adjust camera rotation, drag mouse or scroll

4. When you reach an intersection, the correct color will lead you forward

5. If you reach the end, you win!

6. Lava will restart your progress

7. The maze is randomly generated so if you refresh you can play again on a new maze
